Avoid the traps

The 10 mistakes that kill new cleaning businesses.

These come from real experience. Avoid them and you're already ahead of most people who try this.

01

Advertising before hiring cleaners

If a booking comes in and you have nobody to send — you lose the customer and the trust.

02

Pricing too low to "get clients"

Cheap prices attract cheap customers. You end up working more for less and burning out.

03

Using your personal phone number

No separation. No call recording. No way to hand off to a VA later without disrupting everything.

04

Responding to leads too slowly

On lead platforms, you have a 10-minute window. After that, your conversion rate drops sharply.

05

Accepting cash payments

Cash is untrackable, untaxable chaos that makes your business unscalable. Card only, always.

06

Not calling after the first clean

One follow-up call prevents cancellations, catches issues before they become bad reviews, and builds loyalty.

All 10 mistakes — with their fixes — are covered in full inside the course.
